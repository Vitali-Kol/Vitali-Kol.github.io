What you can expect from us:

You can expect nothing but the best from Rockstar Games: innovative game worlds, compelling stories, and cutting-edge technology that transports players into unique universes. We strive to surprise, inspire, and create experiences that redefine the gaming industry. Every Rockstar game is the result of endless passion, creativity, and a commitment to excellence.

All services
  • World-class video game development: Creating innovative game projects that combine deep storytelling, open world and cutting-edge technology.
  • Game support and updates: Regular patches, content updates and fixes for all current games.
  • Online services and multiplayer: Development and support of popular online platforms such as GTA Online and Red Dead Online, with regular release of new content.
