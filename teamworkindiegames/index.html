ABOUT US

History, ideas, inspiration

Our site is not just an information resource, but a real creative platform for everyone who loves and appreciates indie games. Here you will find fresh reviews, interviews with developers and much more, which helps not only to stay up to date, but also to find inspiration for your own projects.

📜 The history of our journey

2000 — Idea in the Air

A love for indie games and a dream to create a space for like-minded people was born.

2005 — First Steps

The first blog appeared with reviews of little-known but cool projects.

2010 — Recognition in the Community

We started talking about us in the indie community, interviews and collaborations appeared.

2015 — New Level

We launched a full-fledged site, added a forum and a database of indie games.

2020 — Time for Growth

New formats: podcasts, videos, contests. Thousands of new users.

2025 — Indie Eyes of the Future

The site became a media space uniting indie fans and developers.
