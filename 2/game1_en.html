The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Rating: 9.5 / 10 Year: 2015 Developer: CD Projekt Red

Game Description

"The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt" is an epic open-world RPG where you play as Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter for hire. The world is full of mystique, magic, and adventure, with over 100 hours of main storyline and countless side quests that deepen the lore. Explore lands, battle terrifying monsters, complete quests, and make tough decisions that impact character fates and the game’s ending.
