Elden Ring

Rating: 9.7 / 10 Year: 2022 Developer: FromSoftware

Game Description

"Elden Ring" is a dark fantasy action RPG set in an expansive open world, co-created by FromSoftware and author George R. R. Martin. Players explore the Lands Between, overcoming colossal enemies and formidable bosses. Journey across open fields, dungeons, and multi-level castles in a story of ancient power and discovery, combining FromSoftware’s signature gameplay style with a new level of exploration and depth.
