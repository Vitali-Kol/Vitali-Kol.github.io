About Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is a unique hybrid console that lets you play at home on your TV or on the go. With detachable Joy-Con controllers, you can play with a friend on a single screen. The Switch offers a wide library of games, including exclusive Nintendo titles like The Legend of Zelda, Super Mario Odyssey, and many more. It’s ideal for family gaming and entertainment anywhere.
