About Steam Deck

The Steam Deck is a portable gaming console by Valve, designed to play PC games anywhere. It offers access to your Steam library, allowing you to enjoy your favorite games on the go or at home. With its powerful CPU and GPU, the Steam Deck is perfect for modern games and can be used as a fully functional mini-PC with support for peripherals.
