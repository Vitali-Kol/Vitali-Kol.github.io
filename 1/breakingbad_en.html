Breaking Bad

rating: 8.9 / 10 year: 2008 director: Adam Bernstein

Description of Breaking Bad


High school chemistry teacher Walter White learns that he has lung cancer. Considering the difficult financial situation of his family, as well as the prospects, Walter decides to start making methamphetamine. To do this, he recruits his former student Jesse Pinkman, who was once expelled from school with Walter's active assistance. Pinkman himself was engaged in cooking meth, but the day before, during an FBI raid, he lost his partner and laboratory.
