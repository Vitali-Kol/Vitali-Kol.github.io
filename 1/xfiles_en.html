Description of The X-Files


FBI agents Dana Scully and Fox Mulder are assigned to work on the "X-Files" project. This is an archive of unsolved cases related to paranormal phenomena. Mulder believes in aliens and tries to convince skeptic Scully that not everything can be explained rationally. Gradually, their mutual distrust grows into friendship and even deeper feelings.
